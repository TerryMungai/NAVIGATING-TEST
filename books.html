INSIGHTS FROM THE BOOKS

Racism

This is part of the things that seems to repeat itself over and over

around the world, we see from our book TREVOR NOAH the apartheid racism

on Afrikaans word meaning separateness. Under the apartheid, the rights

associations, and movements of the majority black inhabitants and other

ethnic groups were curtailed. The white citizens lived in separate areas than

black citizens, and reserved much less land for the black citizens to live,

despite them making up large majority of the population. The black South Africans

lost their rights as South Africans and were forced to become permanent residents of

the reserves. Another example is use of passes and coordination of documents,

this act forced the black South Africans to carry their passbooks with them at

all times. If someone was found without their passbooks, they could be arrested

for no reason other than that. This caused immense anger within the Black community.
